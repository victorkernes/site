ESPN

Podcast Layout — espn.com/podcast/truehoop

I was a really big fan of the TrueHoop NBA podcast, which was unfortunately shut down this past week. I had created this redesigned layout for the podcast’s page on ESPN.com.

I wanted to focus more on providing a listening experience within the page itself, making it easier for other fans to listen to the latest episode and check out previous ones.

My solution for archiving past episodes may not be the right one, however there does not seem to be anything in place currently. There is still room for improvement in that section.

Please note: I do not own the rights to the ESPN or ESPN Radio logo. I created this project because I’m a fan of the TrueHoop NBA Podcast.
