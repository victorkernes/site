About me

Hello! My name is Victor Kernes Jr. and I am a Marketer/Designer who enjoys working on projects that help solve problems. Currently, I am the Marketing Coordinator at Laurus College where I design and create all of the Email Marketing Campaigns, Social Media Posts, Newspaper Ads, Google Display Ads, Promotional Marketing Materials, and Website Pages.

Every morning I wake up early for three reasons: 1) To drink a cup of coffee. 2) Learn something new by reading a book or blog post. 3) Begin working on my next side project. Now, those aren’t the only things that motivate me to get out of bed, obviously there’s much more to it than that. I extremely value my relationship with God, my wife, and family over anything.

You can usually find me wearing blue and grey almost every day. I once read that the less time spent figuring out what to wear, the more time there is to focus on the things that matter.
